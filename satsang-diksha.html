Satsang Diksha
 
Satsang Diksha

Swaminarayan Bhagwan etle ke sākshāt Akshar Purushottam Maharaj sarvane param shanti, ānand ane sukh arpe. (1)

Ā deha muktinu sādhan chhe, keval bhognu sādhan nathi. Durlabh ane nāshvant evo ādeha vāramvār malto nathi. (2)

Laukik vyavahār to dehnā nirvāha māte chhe. Te ā manushya janma nu param lakshya nathi. (3)

Sarva doshone tālva, brahmasthitine pāmvā ane Bhagwanni bhakti karvā ā deha malyo chhe. Ā badhu satsang karvāthi avashya prāpt thāy chhe. Āthi mumukshoe sadāy satsang karvo. (4-5)

Tethi Parabrahma Swaminarayan e ā lokmā sākshāt avatarine ā divya satsani sthāpnā kari. (6)

Ā satsangnu gnān mumukshuone thāy evā shubh āshaythi 'Satsang Diksha' e nāmnu shāstra rachvāmā āve chhe. (7)

Satya evā ātmāno sang karvo, satya evā Paramātmāno sant karvo, satya evo guruno sang karvo ane sacchhāstrano sang karvo e satsangnu sāchu lakshan jānvu. Āvo divya satsang karnār manushya sukhi thāy chhe. (8-9)

Dikshā etle dradha sankalp, shadhhāe sahit evo achal nischay, samyak sampurna, pratipurvak nishtha, vrat ane dradha āshro. (10)

Ā shāstramā Parabrahma Sahajānand Paramātmāe durshāvel āgnā tathā upāsanāni padhratine spasht rite janāvi chhe. (11)

Purusho tathā strio sarve satsangnā adhikāri chhe, sarve sukhnā adhikāri chhe ane sarve brahmvidyānā adhikāri chhe. (12)

Satsangmā lingbhedthi nyunādhikpanu na j samajvu. Badhā potpotāni maryādāmā rahi bhakti vade muktne pāmi shake chhe. (13)

Sarva varnanā sarva strino tatha sarva purusho sadāy satsang, brahmavidya ane mokshnā adhikāri chhe. Varnanā ādhāre kyāreya nyunādhikbhāv na karvo. Sarva janoe potānā varnanu mān tyajine paraspar sevā karvi. Jātie karine koi mahān nathi ane koi nyun pan nahti. Tehthi nāt-jātne laine klesh na karvo ne sukhe satsang karvo. (14-16)

Gruhastha tathā tyāgi sarva mokshnā adhikāri chhe. Temā nyunādhikbhāv nathi, kāran ke gruhastha ke tyāgi badhā bhagwānnā bhakto chhe. (17)

Swaminarayan Bhagwanne vishe ananya, dradh ane param bhakti māte āshrayadikshāmantra grahan kari satsang prāpt karvo. (18)

Āshrayadikshā mantra ā pramāne chhe -
Dhanyosmi purnākamosmi nishpāpo nirbhayaha sukhi |
Akshar guruyogena Swaminarayanashrayāt || (19)

Mumukshu potānā ātmāni mukti māte Sahajānand Shrihari tathā Aksharbrahma swarup gunātit guruno pritie karine āshro kare. (20)
